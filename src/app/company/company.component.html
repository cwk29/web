About WorTech

Who we are.

The individuals working side-by-side with government, defense, and intelligence community. We bring the experience and expertise needed to deliver the product or service directly to the warfighter. The mission is our #1 priority. Implementing the technological capabilities of tomorrow, today.

Proudly serving the warfighter since 2012.

For more than 10 years, WorTech has successfully delivered custom technology solutions. We deliver quality and bring a unique perspective on future technology to keep clients and stakeholders ahead of the curve.

Our Leaders

We are a company fueled by the people within. Meet the leaders of the WorTech team.

Daniel Wortman

President

Dan is majority owner and founding partner. He is a US Navy veteran, joined the military at 18 years old and then entered the Department of Defense and has over 30 years' experience in this industry. The company previously based in Stevensville MD, moved to Wilmington NC to extend the company's mission focus into North Carolina..

Scott Wecht

Vice President / Technical Director

Scott is WorTech's Technical Director, an Electrical Engineer with over 30 years of practical experience in commercial and government solution spaces - from his early years designing ASICs (“chips”) and coding embedded signal processors, to providing systems engineering for today's container-based microservices architectures - technology has been the driver, but mission remains WorTech's focus. Scott is based out of the company's branch office located in Columbia, MD.

Katie Rahn

Partner Relations & Contracts Representative

Katie brings 15 years of incredible customer relations experience. She is the foundation of WorTech - scheduling, documenting, planning, and managing all aspects of internal and external communications out of WorTech's headquarters stationed in Columbia, MD.
